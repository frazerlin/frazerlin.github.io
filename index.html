Zheng Lin （林 铮）

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Tsinghua University, under the supervision of Prof. Shi-Min Hu. Before that, I get my doctoral degree from Nankai University, under the supervision of Prof. Ming-Ming Cheng. My research interest is in computer vision and graphics related tasks with human interactions. I have published a total of 21 academic papers, with 3 conference papers for oral presentations. My citation in google scholar is 1600+. I serve as a committee member or reviewer for CVPR, ICCV, TPAMI, TIP, etc., and have been awarded outstanding reviewer for CVPR.
