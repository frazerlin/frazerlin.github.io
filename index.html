    Academic Guide

Zheng Lin （林 铮）

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Tsinghua University, under the supervision of Prof. Shi-Min Hu. Before that, I get my doctoral degree from Nankai University in 2023, under the supervision of Prof. Ming-Ming Cheng. My research interest is in computer vision and graphics related tasks with human interactions. I have published a total of 15 academic papers, with 2 conference papers for oral presentations. My citation in google scholar is 1300+. I serve as a committee member or reviewer for CVPR, ICCV, TPAMI, TIP, etc., and have been awarded outstanding reviewer for CVPR.

林铮，清华大学博士后，合作导师为胡事民院士。 博士毕业于南开大学，导师为程明明教授。 主要研究方向为用户参与的计算机视觉与图形学相关任务，共发表了15篇学术论文（7篇为第一作者）， 其中11篇为CCF-A类顶级会议与期刊，包含3篇ESI高倍引论文，1篇ESI热点论文， 2篇一作顶会论文做Oral口头报告（录取率约5%）， 个人谷歌学术引用1300+。 学术服务方面，担任CCF-A类会议与期刊CVPR、ICCV、TPAMI、TIP等程序委员会成员或审稿人，曾获过CVPR杰出审稿人称号等。
